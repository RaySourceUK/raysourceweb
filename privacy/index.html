Privacy Policy

RaySource UK · Last updated: 22 March 2026

TL;DR:

  • We don't require an account, and we don't collect your name, real email address, or any login credentials.
  • We temporarily log your IP address for security and abuse prevention; it's deleted after 1 hour unless you've violated our Terms, in which case it may be retained as a permanent ban.
  • Emails received by your temporary address are stored only to display them to you and are automatically deleted after 1 hour.
  • We don't use cookies, serve ads, run third-party analytics, or sell your data to anyone.
  • Our services are hosted on Cloudflare.
  • UK and EEA users have standard data rights under UK GDPR; contact us at support@raysource.co.uk to exercise them.

For full details, please read the complete policy below.

RaySource UK ("we", "us", "our") are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our Services, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it. We encourage you to read this policy carefully alongside our Terms of Service.

1. Who We Are

RaySource is operated by RaySource UK. Our Services are provided via the website raysource.co.uk. If you have any questions about this policy or our data practices, you can contact us at support@raysource.co.uk.

2. Our Privacy Philosophy

Our Services are designed from the ground up with privacy in mind. We do not require you to create an account, provide your name, or supply a real email address. We collect as little information as is necessary to operate our Services, and we do not sell, rent, or trade any data to third parties.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 IP Addresses

When you use our Services, your IP address is automatically processed by our infrastructure and deleted after 1 hour (60 minutes). We collect and store your IP address for the following purposes:

  • Rate limiting: To prevent abuse of our Services by limiting the number of requests any single user can make within a given time window.
  • Abuse prevention: To detect and block automated bots, scripted attacks, and other misuse of our Services.
  • IP banning: Where a user has violated our Terms of Service, their IP address may be stored with a "banned" flag. These bans are permanent by default to ensure the ongoing security of our infrastructure.
  • IP-to-Email: To enforce our Banned Senders policy, we maintain a temporary link between your IP address and the specific temporary email address you are currently accessing.

Rate-limiting records are stored for a maximum of 60 seconds. Ban records expire after 24 hours unless a longer restriction is applied manually following a serious or repeated violation.

3.2 BurnerMail: Email Data

When an email is received by your temporary address, we store the following information temporarily in order to display it to you:

  • The sender's email address
  • The subject line
  • The raw body of the email
  • A timestamp indicating when the email was received

All email data is automatically and permanently deleted after 1 hour (60 minutes) from the time of receipt. We do not read, analyse, or share the contents of emails for any purpose other than displaying them to you.

3.3 BurnerMail: Filtering & Blacklisting

To protect our Services and Users, we maintain the following restricted lists:

  • Restricted Senders: We maintain a list of known malicious sender addresses. Emails from these addresses are rejected immediately and never stored in our systems.
  • Banned Senders: We maintain a list of prohibited sender domains and specific email addresses (e.g., those associated with unauthorised free-trial creation or prohibited content).

If a User attempts to receive an email from a source on the Banned Senders list, this transaction is flagged. Upon the User's attempt to access the inbox for that specific address, a permanent IP ban is issued to protect our infrastructure and ensure compliance with our BurnerMail Guidelines.

3.4 Abuse Reports

If you submit a report of abusive content via our reporting feature, we store the email identifier, the reason you provided, and your IP address. This information is retained solely for the purpose of reviewing the reported content and taking appropriate action. All reports are deleted after 48 hours.

3.5 What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, real email address, or any account credentials.
  • We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.
  • We do not use third-party analytics services (e.g. Google Analytics).
  • We do not serve advertisements.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

For users in the United Kingdom and European Economic Area, our legal basis for processing your IP address is our legitimate interest in protecting the integrity and security of our Services, and in preventing abuse. This processing is proportionate to the risks involved and does not override your rights and freedoms.

5. Data Retention

The following retention periods apply to data we process:

  • Rate-limiting records: Deleted automatically after 60 seconds.
  • IP Address: Deleted automatically after 1 hour, unless the IP is flagged for a violation, in which case it is retained as an IP ban record.
  • Temporary email data: Deleted automatically after 1 hour from the time of receipt.
  • IP-to-Email: Deleted automatically after 1 hour (60 minutes), tied to the lifespan of temporary email data, unless a violation triggers a permanent IP ban.
  • IP ban records: Retained indefinitely to ensure the ongoing security and integrity of our Service. These records are only reviewed or removed upon a successful manual appeal.
  • Abuse reports: Deleted automatically after 48 hours to allow for administrative review.

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties, except in the following limited circumstance:

  • Legal obligations: We may disclose information if required to do so by law, regulation, or court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of RaySource UK, our users, or others.

6.1 Infrastructure Providers

Our Services are hosted on Cloudflare infrastructure. Data processed through our Services passes through Cloudflare's global network to ensure security and speed. Cloudflare operates under its own Privacy Policy, and use of their services is strictly for the technical delivery and protection of RaySource.

7. Your Rights

If you are located in the United Kingdom or the European Economic Area, you have the following rights in relation to the personal data we hold about you:

  • Right of access: You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to erasure: You have the right to request that we delete your personal data, subject to any legal obligations we have to retain it.
  • Right to object: You have the right to object to our processing of your data on the basis of legitimate interests.
  • Right to restriction: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your data in certain circumstances.

Please note that because we collect very limited personal data and most of it expires automatically within minutes or hours, it may not be possible to identify or retrieve your specific records by the time a request is received.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@raysource.co.uk.

8. Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Our Services are delivered over HTTPS, and data is stored using Cloudflare's Key-Value infrastructure with strict access controls.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us at support@raysource.co.uk and we will take steps to delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our Services after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests relating to this Privacy Policy or the way we handle your data, please contact us at:

support@raysource.co.uk
